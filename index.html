Life Philosophy

My perspective on dreams, aims, and reality

I see dreams, aims, and goals differently than how we were taught. As kids, we'd say "I want to be a doctor, an engineer, an IAS officer" - but we didn't truly understand those professions. We were attracted to the idea, the respect, the uniform, the lifestyle.

Over time, I realized: those titles aren't dreams. They're just possible paths.

🎯 The Dream

A dream isn't a profession - it's the life you want to live. For me: comfort, freedom, a gaming sanctuary, the ability to travel, financial stability, and living on my own terms. That's the dream.

🧭 The Aim

An aim is the direction toward that life. Not a fixed profession, but an evolving path as I learn more about myself and the world. Flexibility is strength.

⚙️ The Work

Goals and work are the tools to reach the dream. A job, business, online work - whatever builds the life I envision. The methods change, but the destination remains.

"If you ask me what I'll do in life, I'm figuring it out. The tools may change, but the dream stays the same."

Sometimes, a man gets exactly what he wishes for, and that can be the most perfect punishment of all.
